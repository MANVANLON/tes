Pricing

We offer affordable prices and we have no hidden charges. We do not charge for flights of stairs, No fuel or Mileage charge. We are fully insured, covering you up to £50,000 (goods in transit)

Option 1 – Hourly Rate (Minimum Booking of 2 Hours)

  • Van and Man – from £50 per hour
  • Van and 2 Men – from £65 per hour
  • Van and 3 Men – from £80 per hour (minimum 3 hours)

This is ideal for a small amount of items or office removals and a very cost-effective way of getting your items moved from A to B with minimum fuss.

Option 2 – Half Day Rate

  • Van and 1 Man – from £195
  • Van and 2 Men – from £250
  • Van and 3 Men – from £320

If you think your move might take over 4 hours then you will save money by booking us by the half day.  A half day is up to four hours.

Option 3 – Full Day Rate

  • Van and 1 Man for a full day – from £390
  • Van and 2 Men for a full day – from £495
  • Van and 3 Men for a full day – from £645

A full day is up to 8 hours with your van and team.  The most cost effective rate for a full house or flat move.  If you are unsure as to how much time your move will take then call us with your requirements and we will provide you with a quote.

Option 4 – Whatever you need!

If your requirements don’t match any of the above then we would be delighted to discuss your requirements with you.  Call us any time and we will be more than happy to give you a price for your removal needs.

We are happy to pick up and drop off at any time of the day – or night!  We can keep items secure for next day delivery and you can rest assured that your items are safe and secure and covered by our insurance.

Don’t forget, all of our prices are VAT free and there are no hidden charges or costs to consider.

For anything and everything else call us for a quote on 1111111111.
