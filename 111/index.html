The Best Value in London

When you are looking for the best solution to move house or flat, have bulky items delivered or removed, or your business needs items clearing or taking to a new location then we believe that a man and van service is the best value for money solution.  Self drive van hire is expensive, time-consuming and more likely to result in delays or accidents.  Our drivers are all CRB checked and our vans and your contents are fully insured.  Our drivers know the London streets and can avoid major congestion areas and are used to driving larger vehicles.

Our man and van services can also include extra helpers, who are charged by the hour.  So if you have a lot of items to move it is more cost effective to hire the van with the extra man-power to enable us to move your items as quickly, and as cheaply, as possible.

There are no hidden charges with our services, there is no VAT to add on to the total prices, we do NOT charge extra for fuel and the insurance is all covered in the costs that we provide.
